Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 12, 2025

1. Introduction

We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and strive to be transparent about how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. This privacy policy outlines our practices when you visit our website or engage with our related services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in several ways, including:

  • Requesting a cash offer for your property
  • Subscribing to our newsletter or email list
  • Contacting us with inquiries or requests

The types of personal information we collect may include:

  • Full name
  • Contact information (email, phone number, address)
  • Information about your property
  • Any other information you choose to provide

We also collect some information automatically when you visit our site, including:

  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Browsing history
  • Cookies and usage data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process your requests for cash offers
  • Communicate with you, including via SMS if you opt-in
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Market our services, where permitted by law

4. How We Share Your Information

  • Service providers and partners who assist us with business operations
  • Legal authorities when required by law or to protect our rights
  • Affiliated entities or during business transfers

5. 10DLC Compliance

In compliance with 10DLC regulations:

  • We use your phone number only for sending SMS messages related to our services.
  • You can opt-out at any time.
  • We maintain records of your consent and SMS communications as required.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your data
  • Restriction on processing and portability of data
  • Objection to processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us.

7. Data Retention

We will retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy.

8. Security

We implement security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on our website.

10. Compliance Statements

We are committed to compliance with relevant data protection laws such as GDPR and CCPA.

11. User Education

We encourage users to educate themselves about privacy rights and best practices for online security.

12. Accessibility

We strive to ensure accessibility for users with disabilities by providing alternative formats and assistive technologies.

13. Messaging and Communication

By providing your contact information, you consent to receive messages from CBB Home Buyers. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on user interactions.

We respect your privacy and do not share customer phone numbers with third parties.
